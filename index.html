About Chai Theory

Brewing stories, one cup at a time

Chai Theory was born out of a simple idea — to bring people together over a cup of chai. Inspired by the warmth of traditional dhabas and blended with a modern café experience, we’ve created a space that feels like home but tastes extraordinary.

From soulful doodh pati to crisp parathas, and from late-night fries to cozy wraps, every item on our menu is crafted to spark conversations, laughter, and memories.
